My First Blog-Poem

敕勒川，阴山下，天似穹庐，笼盖四野，天苍苍，野茫茫，风吹草底现牛（🐂）羊（🐏)

This poem describes so meone living or seeing a Mongolian Plain. There is a mountain overhead the plains.The sky is like a giant dome overhead, It covers the entirety of the plains. The skyline is massive and the plains seem to never end, and when the wind blows the grass uncovers the sheep and cows that were in the grass.
